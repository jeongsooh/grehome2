About

Our achievements to date.

  • 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Got together for new businesses in energy sector. Specialized in real-time power measurements and data analytics

  • July 2021

    The first EV charging outlet, CP100 in the market.

    More than 80% people lives in apartment complex in Korea and home charging needs specialized outlet box which supports authorization and power measurement.

    As of today, more than 5,000 CP100 have been installed in Korea.

  • May 2023

    AC wallbox EV charger CP700

    Based on market requirements, developed 7kW AC charger, but we realized that smart charging will become an essential feature in years to come.

  • May 2024

    Next Generation AC charger CP700P in the market

    Introduced AC Smart charger CP700P in the market, which supports ISO 15118-2 AC charging as well as PWM signaling, thru PLC modem.

    The first commercial installation was started in Dec. 2024 and more than 2,000 CP700P runs as of today.
